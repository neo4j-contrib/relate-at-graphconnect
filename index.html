Relate! with your fellow graphistas

Like trading the strict schema of relational tables for the organic organization of a graph, the Relate! hands-on track at GraphConnect will guide community-driven participatory events during the conference. We'll provide demo space, organize working sessions, and host social discussions as you establish your own connections with other graphistas.

Register today for GraphConnect, then Relate! with us.
